Frequently Asked Questions

What is Perfume Picks?

Perfume Picks is an iOS app for fragrance collectors. You catalog your wardrobe, log every wear, and get weather- and occasion-aware picks from the bottles you already own. A taste profile is built from your real collection — not just a quiz.

Is Perfume Picks a retailer?

No. Perfume Picks does not sell fragrance. When you want to buy a bottle, the app links out to retailers like FragranceX, Sephora, or Amazon Luxury Beauty. We may earn a small commission on those links at no cost to you, and this is disclosed in the Terms.

Is Perfume Picks free? What does Pro unlock?

The app is free to download. Free gives you the basic taste quiz, a limited daily round of Train My Nose swipes, and a wardrobe of up to twenty bottles. Pro unlocks the full quiz, unlimited swipes, the taste profile screen, advanced analytics, cost-per-wear, and Perfume Wrapped. Pro is monthly or yearly, managed through Apple, and cancelable anytime in iOS Settings.

How does the wear log work?

Tap a fragrance you wore today and confirm. The app records the date, captures local weather automatically, and asks you for occasion and how many sprays. You can backdate up to two years, log multiple fragrances on the same day for layering experiments, and search wears by season, occasion, or fragrance family later on.

Is my wardrobe data private?

Yes. Your wardrobe, wear log, ratings, and notes are private by default. Nothing is shared, sold, or used for cross-user ad targeting. Scent of the Day posts become public only when you flip the toggle on a specific entry. See the Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.

Will there be an Android version?

Perfume Picks launches on iOS first. An Android release is on the roadmap but not yet scheduled.

Why an app instead of a website?

A wardrobe lives in your pocket. Logging a wear should take three taps from a notification, not a desktop login. The phone also gives us camera-based bottle entry, local weather, and the kind of quick swipe interface that builds a real taste profile over time.
