Features

Everything Perfume Picks does, in one place.

Morning Pick

What should I wear today?

The morning pick draws from the bottles you already own. It considers local weather, the occasion you are heading into, the family you have been wearing too much of, and bottles you have not touched in weeks.

Discovery is fine, but the everyday question is "what do I wear right now?" — and the answer should come from your shelf, not a shopping cart.

Wardrobe

Your collection, perfectly organized

Every bottle in one place. Track each fragrance by house, family, and status — have, want, worn, or tested. A remaining-mL meter shows how much is left as you log wears.

Record purchase price per bottle. Filter by season, family, or status. Sort by what you have worn least recently, so the back of the shelf gets its turn.

Taste Profile

Train My Nose — built from real behavior

A swipe interface presents scents with their notes, families, and houses. Swipe right on what you love, down to bookmark, left to pass. Your profile starts to take shape within a few rounds.

The profile sharpens with every wear you log. Built from your real collection and your real choices — not from a five-question quiz.

Discover

Find your next bottle

Curated edits by mood — Boudoir, Office, Date Night — alongside celebrity favorites and new arrivals. Browse by house, family, or occasion.

Every fragrance links out to retailers so you can buy when you are ready. No shopping pressure, no algorithmic push — just a well-organized catalog.

Fragrance Detail

Every note, every wear

Tap any fragrance to see its full note pyramid, concentration, release year, and your personal wear history. See the last day you wore it and how it performs across occasions.

When you are ready to buy, "Buy from" links connect you directly to retailers — with affiliate disclosure right on the page.

Analytics

Cost-per-wear and Perfume Wrapped

Every wear ticks the counter on the bottle's purchase price. The expensive bottle you reach for daily quietly becomes the cheap one. The niche purchase you have worn three times reveals itself.

At year-end, Perfume Wrapped recaps your year — top houses, top families, total wears, layering favorites, and the most-loved bottle by wears per month. Sharable as an image card.
