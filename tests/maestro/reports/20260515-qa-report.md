## Highest-Priority Findings

1. **P1 — Brand name truncation persists.** "OLFACTIVE STUDIO" still shows as "OLFACTIVE S..." on compact cards. The eyebrow at 11pt with letter-spacing is still too wide for long brand names. Fix: either reduce letter-spacing on the eyebrow, or show brand name without ALL CAPS for brands > 15 chars.

2. **P1 — Fragrance names too verbose from catalog.** The Supabase catalog has pipe-separated names like "Dancing Light | Eau de Parfum 100ml | Jasmine | Neroli | Musks". This is raw catalog data that should be cleaned — only the fragrance name should display in cards, not the full product listing string. Fix: strip everything after the first `|` for card display.

3. **P2 — RevenueCat error banner.** "Error configuring Purchases: Error: Invalid A..." shows at the bottom of the login screen and persists into the app. Expected in simulator without StoreKit config, but should be suppressed or dismissed in production.

4. **P2 — Splash screenshot timing.** The first screenshot captures the splash animation overlay rather than the home screen. Maestro needs a longer wait or a post-splash assertion before the first screenshot.

5. **P2 — Tab navigation via Maestro.** Point-based taps on the tab bar are fragile and miss on iPhone 17 Pro dimensions. The flows need accessibility labels on tab bar items, or use `scrollUntilVisible` + text tap approaches.
