## Known Limitations (Not Bugs)

- **Fragrance detail navigation**: Maestro cannot reliably tap into fragrance detail pages from rails (hero card content is dynamic, coordinate taps fragile). Detail page QA requires manual testing or dedicated deep-link flows.
- **DiscoverFilterSheet draft state**: Draft doesn't re-sync from filters prop when sheet reopens (only initializes on first `useState`). Documented for future fix.
- **CelebritySection image_url**: Field selected from Supabase but `Image` component not rendered when `image_url` is set. Cosmetic — falls back gracefully.
