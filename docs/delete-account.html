Delete Your Account

You can delete your Perfume Picks account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1 — Delete From Inside the App (recommended)

  1. Open Perfume Picks on your iPhone.
  2. Sign in with the account you want to delete.
  3. Tap the Profile tab.
  4. Tap Settings.
  5. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm by tapping Delete.

Your account and all data will be removed immediately.

Option 2 — Email Request

If you have already uninstalled the app or cannot access it, send an email to bguillow@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. Include the subject line "Delete My Account".

Account deletion typically completes within 24 to 48 hours, and always within 7 days. You will receive a confirmation when deletion is complete.

What gets deleted

  • Your sign-in identity (Apple or Google).
  • Your profile (display name, avatar, taste profile).
  • Your wardrobe (every fragrance entry with status, remaining mL, and purchase price).
  • Your wear log (every wear with date, occasion, weather, and notes).
  • Your Train My Nose swipe history.
  • Your quiz results.
  • Any ratings, private notes, and reviews you submitted.
  • Your Scent of the Day posts and your follow / follower relationships.

What is retained

For legal, billing, and fraud-prevention purposes we may retain:

  • Anonymized purchase records (held by Apple).
  • Anonymized aggregate analytics that cannot be traced back to you — for example, that someone in your country logged a wear on a given day, with no identifier attached.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription. You must cancel the subscription separately in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or it will continue to renew.
